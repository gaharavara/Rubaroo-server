About Us

Rubaroo, the cultural extravaganza is not merely a fest but a platform to stand out from the rest and let your inner self out. It is an occasion to let bonds build, to let every individual evolve, to create memories and cherish every moment.

Every year Rubaroo seeks to create an everlasting experience for everyone, then be it its events, fun events or pro nights. With artists like Bohe-mia, Tochi Rana, Javed Ali, Eka Band, Euphoria Band, Indian Ocean Vh1 supersonic, Sunburn, Darshan Raval who have already graced this festival with their mind boggling performances, this year we are back again with an upgraded level of excitement and thrill.

Being held on the 27th and 28th of September, RUBAROO’19 is all set to blow your minds. From fashion to drama, dance to music, fun events to literary events, every part of this festival ensures to create a mesmerizing impression on your minds.
